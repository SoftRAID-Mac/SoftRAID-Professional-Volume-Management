## What is SoftRAID?

![SoftRAID Volume Window](https://www.storagenewsletter.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/SoftRAID_Interface.jpg)

SoftRAID is a comprehensive and powerful utility designed for professionals who require the highest level of performance and data protection from their storage systems. It stands as the leading software solution for creating and managing RAID disk arrays, offering unparalleled control and reliability beyond what is available in standard operating system tools.

This advanced application provides a complete suite of features for disk management. You can create, test, and repair RAID 1, 4, 5, 1+0, and 5+0 volumes with ease. A key strength of **SoftRAID professional volume management** is its predictive drive failure technology. ## Tips & Tricks

- Regularly use the "Verify Volume" feature to ensure data integrity
- Enable SMART monitoring to receive early warnings about potential drive failures
- For best performance, use identical drives when creating a new RAID array
- Schedule volume verification during periods of low activity
